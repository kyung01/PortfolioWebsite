These are my early games that helped me to get started. Each of them do not stand alone as a presentable material. So I have combined all of them to showcase my history.

Laser Firing Flying Cat Game

Made with Eclipse. I have learned basics of creating Android application; such as SDK, API, and rendering methods.

Flood It Game With Two Modes (limited time or limited number of steps)

This is traditional "flood it" game with two modes. I have tried to make infinitely scaling flood it game. I made it without concept of memory and such. The result was a game that slowly killed itself by consumming too much memory as you played more and more.

Connect Dots Within The Circle Game

I became interested in better looking game. In this game I have tried to create randomly generated tree pattern and procedually generated level in terms of dots. The game was slow and boring. However it was the prettiest game I have made and most stable game than the ones before.

Slide Shapes To Fit Into Given Example Shape

In this game, you slice a given shape on your screen to fit into the shape displayed behind.

It was the most technical game I have written at the time. I had to learn indices, vertices, and buffer. Besides the technical details, the math behind it was a great challenge at the time because I didn't have a strong math background. This was a project that taught me the importance of math.
